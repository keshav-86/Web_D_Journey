Burfi

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • pistachios
  • Sugar
  • cardamom
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Firstly, in a bowl take 2½ cup milk powder, ¾ cup sugar and 1 cup milk.
  2. whisk and mix well until everything is well combined.
  3. Also add ¼ cup ghee and mix well on low flame.
  4. The mixture will hold the shape and starts to separate the pan after 10 minutes.
  5. Top with few chopped pistachios and cut with sharp knife.
